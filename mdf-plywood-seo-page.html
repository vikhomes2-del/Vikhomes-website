Why MDF Board?

MDF is one of the most widely used engineered wood products in modern furniture production. It is smooth, strong, easy to machine, and perfect for wardrobes, kitchen cabinets, bed frames, dressing tables, shelves, and interiors.

Why Marine Plywood?

Marine plywood is designed to resist water and humidity, making it ideal for kitchens, bathrooms, outdoor furniture, and heavy‑use areas. True marine plywood lasts longer, holds screws better, and reduces long‑term repair costs.
